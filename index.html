Technology-Enhanced Learning

Final ITEL Report Released

The three-year Initiative for Technology-Enhanced learning supported 162 projects involving 233 different faculty at Georgetown. Read more about the projects, outcomes, and recommendations in the final ITEL report at itel.georgetown.edu

Canvas Adoption Spreads across the University

Faculty across Georgetown are choosing Canvas as their course management system. With 24/7 support, a clean and intuitive interface, and quick and easy grading tools, there’s a lot to like. | Learn more about Canvas at Georgetown
